Should I outsource QA testing or hire in-house?

+
If you're a startup or mid-size company that needs quality assurance but can't justify a full-time hire, outsourcing QA is the smarter move. With a QA outsourcing partner like goGreenlit, you get senior-level software testing services without the overhead of salaries, benefits, and management. What QA testing tools and frameworks do you use?

+
We work with the tools your engineering team already uses. For test management, that includes JIRA, TestRail, and Zephyr. For API testing, we use Postman and Swagger. Do I need QA testing for my startup?

+
If your startup is shipping software to real users, you need quality assurance.
